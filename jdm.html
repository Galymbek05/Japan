JDM

Japanese Domestic Market

JDM stands for "Japanese Domestic Market." It refers to the Japanese market for vehicles, parts, and accessories that are specifically designed and produced for the domestic consumption in Japan. JDM is often used to describe vehicles that were originally manufactured for the Japanese market and may have certain unique features or specifications that differ from their counterparts in other markets. These vehicles are sometimes imported and modified by car enthusiasts in other countries who appreciate the distinctive style, performance, or engineering of JDM vehicles. JDM culture has gained popularity worldwide, particularly among car enthusiasts who admire the Japanese automotive industry and its unique offerings. It encompasses various aspects such as modified cars, aftermarket parts, tuning, racing, and the overall aesthetic associated with JDM vehicles. Overall, JDM refers to the Japanese market and the culture surrounding vehicles and automotive enthusiasts associated with it.
